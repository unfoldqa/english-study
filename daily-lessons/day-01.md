# День 1 - урок на 20 минут

## 0-3 мин: Разогрев

Ответь на английском:

1. How are you feeling today?
2. What did you do this morning?
3. What do you want to improve in English?

## 3-8 мин: Лексика

- improve
- confident
- habit
- goal
- practice
- focus
- mistake
- explain
- natural
- fluency

## 8-13 мин: Грамматика (просто)

Present Simple = обычные действия
- I drink coffee every morning.

Present Continuous = действие прямо сейчас
- I am drinking coffee now.

Формула:
- Present Simple: I/you/we/they + verb; he/she/it + verb+s
- Present Continuous: am/is/are + verb-ing

## 13-18 мин: Практика

Выбери правильный вариант:

1. I (study / am studying) English every day.
2. We (drink / are drinking) coffee right now.
3. He (watches / is watching) TV at the moment.
4. They (go / are going) to work on weekdays.
5. I (feel / am feeling) tired now.

## 18-20 мин: Разговорный блок

Скажи 5 предложений:
- 2 в Present Simple
- 2 в Present Continuous
- 1 с новым словом
